The People & The Beauty

Uganda is defined by its soul. It is a place where 56 tribes harmonize, creating a culture built on the foundation of "Ubuntu"—the belief that we are because you are. From the chaotic, colorful energy of Kampala to the silent, golden savannahs of the North, the beauty of the landscape is only matched by the warmth of its people.

Respecting the Culture

The Right Hand

Use your right hand for everything—eating, giving, and greeting. It is the hand of honor.

Modesty

Ugandans value smartness. In rural areas, keeping knees covered shows respect to elders.

Hospitality

If offered food, accept a small amount. It is the ultimate sign of friendship.

Greetings

Never rush. A simple "Osiibye otyanno" (How is your day?) opens every door.
