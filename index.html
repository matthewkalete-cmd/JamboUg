Cultural Wisdom

Traveling with Respect

Handshakes

Use the right hand for greetings and exchanges. It is the hand of honor and respect.

Modesty

Ugandans value appearance. Modest dress is appreciated when visiting rural homes.

Grace

Always accept a small portion of food if offered; it is the ultimate sign of friendship.

Patience

Greetings aren't just words; they are an investment in the day. Take your time.
